PSNA College Of Engineering And Technology


Kalvithanthai Shri. R. S. Kothandaraman, Visionary, Philanthropist, Educationist and Pioneer chose the location on Dindigul - Palani Highway in TamilNadu, India and established the PSNA College of Engineering & Technology in 1984 with a vision to provide world class engineering education. It is a quality technical institution matching International standards. It has been making rapid strides in all fronts and setting new benchmarks.

i.Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
ii.Accredited with ‘A’ grade by NAAC.
iii.All UG Programmes along with MBA and MCA are accredited by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi.
iv. 7 U.G programmes
v. 9 P.G programmes (including MCA and MBA)
vi. Outcome based education to develop graduates with desirable professional attributes.
vii. Research Excellence in all Departments being recognized as research centres by Anna University, Chennai.


PSNA I-Math Club 2022


The Department of Mathematics conducts interdepartmental activities like Math’s quiz, Aptitude test, etc to develop the mathematical skills of the students. Utmost care is taken by the faculty members of the department to bridge the curriculum and modern needs. In the year 2015-2016, maths club was renamed as i-math club(infinity math) with an intension to extend the maths club activities infinitely. In addition to all the above events i-math club of PSNACET conducted various inter departmental competitions like aptitude test, Maths quiz ,wherein the students expressed their mathematical skills. We focus on helping out the student to develop mathematical skills
